01 — Premium skips Southeast Asia

No premium flag planted in SEA yet

No major premium C-beauty brand has committed to Vietnam, Malaysia or Indonesia. Florasis and Winona went to Japan, Europe and the US first — the region's premium shelf is still wide open.

02 — Indonesia is the battleground

Seven mass brands, 15%+ of the category

Focallure, Skintific, Y.O.U, Dazzle Me, Pinkflash, Barenbliss and O.TWO.O helped Chinese brands take over 15% of Indonesia's mass colour-cosmetics market by 2024, up from about 2% in 2019.

03 — Vietnam is the fast lane

The default first stop for makeup

Perfect Diary, Colorkey and Joy Group's brands all point to Vietnam as a top or first-choice Southeast Asian market — frequently via TikTok Shop specifically.
